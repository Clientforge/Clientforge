Automations

What gets automated.

Every workflow runs in the background so your team can focus on clients in the chair — not chasing texts and voicemails.

AI Missed Call Response

Every missed call gets an instant intelligent reply — answers questions, shares your booking link, and captures the lead automatically.

Appointment Reminders

Confirmation plus 72hr, 24hr, and 2hr reminder sequence cuts no-shows before they happen.

Review Requests

Post-visit text sent automatically while the experience is still fresh — builds your Google rating on autopilot.

Cancellation Recovery
