Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using our platform, including name, email address, phone number, and other contact details. This information is submitted through our website, contact forms, integrated forms, and the websites and integrations of businesses you contact.

SMS and Text Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number through our platform, our website, the websites of businesses you contact, contact forms, landing pages, or integrated forms (including ads and inquiry forms), you consent to receive SMS text messages from ClientForge.ai and the businesses you contacted regarding your inquiry, follow-up communications, and related service communications.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Consent is collected at the point where you submit your phone number. Each submission constitutes your agreement to receive SMS communications.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide appointment scheduling and follow-up communications, respond to your inquiries, and improve our platform. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Opt-Out and Data Rights

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
