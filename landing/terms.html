Program Name

ClientForge.ai SMS Messaging Program

Program Description

ClientForge.ai provides SMS messaging services to appointment-based businesses (such as med spas, salons, and clinics). When you submit your phone number through a contact form, landing page, or inquiry form on a business's website, you consent to receive text messages from that business regarding your inquiry, appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-up communications. ClientForge.ai is the technology platform that delivers these messages on behalf of the businesses you contact.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier rates apply to all SMS messages sent and received. You are responsible for any charges from your mobile carrier. ClientForge.ai and the businesses using our platform do not charge you for receiving messages, but your carrier may.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the business. You may receive messages when you submit an inquiry, when an appointment is confirmed or reminded, and for follow-up communications. Message frequency is typically low and tied to your specific requests and appointments.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time.

  • Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive a confirmation message, and no further messages will be sent.
  • Reply HELP to any message for assistance or to request support contact information.

Opt-out requests are processed promptly. After opting out, you will not receive further SMS messages unless you provide your phone number again and consent to receive messages.
