SMS Consent

How you opt in to receive text messages — Last updated: March 2025

How Consent Is Collected

End users opt in by providing their phone number through:

  • Contact forms — On the websites of appointment-based businesses (med spas, salons, clinics) you contact
  • Landing pages — Inquiry or appointment request forms
  • Integrated forms — Forms on ads or partner sites where you request information or schedule an appointment

Consent is collected at the point of submission when you enter your phone number and submit the form. Each submission constitutes your agreement to receive SMS about your inquiry, appointment scheduling, reminders, and follow-up communications.

What You'll Receive

Messages are sent by the businesses you contacted regarding:

  • Confirmation of your inquiry
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Follow-up communications

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier rates apply. You can opt out at any time.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.
