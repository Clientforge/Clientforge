SMS from Client Forge (This Program)

When you provide your phone number to ClientForge.ai on our contact form or through our website or app, you expressly agree to receive SMS text messages from ClientForge.ai only. Consent is between you and ClientForge.ai; it is not shared with or transferred to other companies for them to message you under this consent. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance.

What You May Receive from ClientForge.ai

  • Follow-ups on your inquiry or demo request
  • Product, onboarding, and account-related communications
  • Service updates relevant to your relationship with Client Forge

Opt-In Language (Point of Collection)

The following language appears on our contact form when you submit your phone number:

"I agree to receive SMS text messages from ClientForge.ai regarding my inquiry, Client Forge product and account communications, and follow-ups. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out."

This consent is shown alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms. It applies only to SMS sent by ClientForge.ai under this program.

Our Platform and Your Customers’ End Users

ClientForge.ai also provides software so appointment-based businesses can message their own clients. SMS to those end users is sent by each business under its own brand and registration; each business must collect its own consent. That workflow is separate from the SMS program described on this page. Consent given to Client Forge does not authorize your business or any third party to text consumers using that same consent.
